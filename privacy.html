Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Summary

NCLEX Pulse is built to respect your privacy. The App works without an account and keeps your study data on your device. We do not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

The App does not require a name, email, or login to use. Your progress, streaks, badges, and mastery stats are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple shares only the anonymous entitlement status needed to unlock Premium.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. If you have diagnostics sharing enabled, Apple may share anonymized crash reports with us through App Store Connect.

Data Security

Because your study data stays on your device, it is protected by your device’s security. Deleting the App removes this local data.

Children’s Privacy

NCLEX Pulse is intended for nursing students and professionals and is not directed at children under 13.

Your Choices

You can reset local progress from within the App and manage purchase access through your Apple ID. Deleting the App stops all local data storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email support@aclsmed.com.
